# Code of Conduct

## Our standards

We welcome respectful, constructive collaboration. Be considerate, discuss ideas rather than people, respect privacy and consent, and make space for different backgrounds and experience levels.

Harassment, discrimination, threats, sexualized conduct, sustained disruption, doxxing, and sharing another person's credentials or private data are not acceptable. Do not ask anyone to post credentials in an issue or pull request.

## Enforcement

Report conduct concerns privately through the repository's moderation and reporting tools. Maintainers may edit or remove content, close contributions, restrict participation, or ban accounts when needed to protect the community. Enforcement decisions will consider context, impact, and repeated behavior.
